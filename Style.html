Reviews of some movies


Tanhanji : The unsung warrier


Cast and acting :

Casting includes Ajay Devgan in title role and Saif ali khan as antagonist Udaybhan Rathod. Both main leads play the role to perfection. Saif Ali Khan struggles with Rajput accent but overall his performance is praiseworthy. Also supporting cast including Sharad Kelkar as ‘Shivaji Maharaj’ and kajol as ‘Savtri’ puts forth brilliant performances.

Direction and screenplay :

Director Om Raut does full justice the story and the most commendable aspect of the movie is the screenplay which invests you in the movie fully. Action scenes and battle sequences in the movie are well choreographed and will keep you at edge of your seats. This movie is not so realistic and takes some cinematic liberties for presenting the story. Some action scenes seems larger than life but it is okay for the movie which is presented at this pitch. As a entertainer this movie fabulous.

Songs and background music :

In the overall impact created by the film background music of the movie takes the large share. Music of the movie created by Ajay-Atul and Sachet-Parampara is very impactful and will give you goosebumps at several moments. The songs of the movie sometimes feels unnecessary but they doesn’t affect the flow of the movie.

CGI and costume design:

The costume design of the movie is period accurate and plays the important role of transferring the audience to the era. Visual effects of the movie are just fantastic. The movie is presented at grand scale and visual team does full justice to the movie and era. The film is visual treat for the eyes.

Uri : The sergical strike



1. Storyline and screenplay :

Although the story is inspired by true events it has been dramatized for filmy liberty. But overall the story is catchy and it will not disappoint you on any level. The screenplay is also very good. Also there are no unnecessary songs in the movie ,no romantic scenes and also there is not unnecessary cringy comedy sequences.

2. Acting and characters :

The characters in the movie are developed so well in the movie that we can’t even tell which is real and which is fictious character. Every actor in the film plays respective role so perfectly that you couldn’t imagine any other actor doing same role. Vicky Kaushal plays the role of major vihan shergil with such a perfection that you will forget this is the same guy who played Kamli in ‘Sanju’. Also other supporting cast Mohit Raina, Paresh Rawal, Yami Gautam, Kriti Kulhari in their respective roles does a great job.

3. Music :

The music in the movie plays very impactful role in building up the sequence. Music director Shashwat Sachdeva does wonderful job .every action sequence in movie comes with this impactful and motivational music. You can feel the goosebumps in the movie theatre while watching this sequences.

4. Action and combat sequences:

The main aspect of any army based movie is its action and combat sequences. This combat sequqnces, whether they are crossfiring with guns or hand to hand combat, they are made with perfection and realism that you must experience in theatre only.
At last this movie directed by Aditya Dhar, produced by Ronnie Screwvala is must watch for any Indian. This movie makes you proud of Indian army.
